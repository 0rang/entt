1 # EnTT in Action
2 
3 `EnTT` is widely used in private and commercial applications. I cannot even
4 mention most of them because of some signatures I put on some documents time
5 ago. Fortunately, there are also people who took the time to implement open
6 source projects based on `EnTT` and did not hold back when it came to
7 documenting them.
8 
9 Below an incomplete list of games, applications and articles that can be used as
10 a reference. Where I put the word _apparently_ means that the use of `EnTT` is
11 documented but the authors didn't make explicit announcements or contacted me
12 directly.
13 
14 I hope this list can grow much more in the future:

16 * Games:

38  playground.
39  * [EnTT Tower Defense](https://github.com/Daivuk/tddod): a data oriented tower
40  defense example.
41  * [EnTT Breakout](https://github.com/vblanco20-1/entt-breakout): simple
42  example of a breakout game, using `SDL` and `EnTT`.
43  * [Arcade puzzle game with EnTT](https://github.com/MasonRG/ArcadePuzzleGame):
44  arcade puzzle game made in C++ using the `SDL2` and `EnTT` libraries.
45  * [Snake with EnTT](https://github.com/MasonRG/SnakeGame): simple snake game
46  made in C++ with the `SDL2` and `EnTT` libraries.
